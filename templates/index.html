Establish your Country

Build your nation from the ground up. Collect taxes from your citizens, declare war on enemies, and establish and economy, selling and buying resources on a global market. Compete with real people to become the best.

Join a Coalition

Work with friends to build dominant factions. Engage in politics with allies; trade and establish treaties to rise to the top. Raid countries together, share resources, and fight in vigorous battles agianst other coalitions.

Customize your Economy

Resource Production

Start off producing resources, like food, oil, coal, uranium, wood, and much more, and sell them on the market to other players.

Eventually build up a strong economy to support production of advanced resources, such as components, steel, ammunition, aluminium, and gasoline.

Tax Collection

Try to keep your people happy, healthy and fed. Build stores

Once you can produce enough money, purchase consumer goods and provide enough to your population to make more money.

War Looting

Brandsh your firearms and wage war against other players. Build up tanks, bombers, destroyers, and submarines to crush your enemies and steal their resources

When you have enough resources, build up missiles and nukes and send them aloft to watch your adversaries burn.

Join our Community

Head over to our Discord server to chat with our community, and check out our Reddit and Twitter as well! You can find a coalition to join in our Discord, and you can stay up to date on changes through our Twitter.
